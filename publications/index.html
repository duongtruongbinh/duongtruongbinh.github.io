    Truong-Binh Duong, Hoang-Minh Tran, Binh-Nam Le-Nguyen, and 1 more author
    Proc. ICISN 2025 (Springer LNNS), 2025

    Yen-Linh Vu*, Dinh-Thang Duong*, Truong-Binh Duong, and 1 more author
    ICCV Workshop, 2025
    *Equal contribution
